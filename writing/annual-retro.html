Annual Retro

Five prompts, ranked by impact (easy first), to review by yourself, your family, your team, etc. These prompts sit alongside goal review and planning.

Historian

Ten years from now, you look back at this year, what's the headline? What's the story of the year?

Consultant

What were your three biggest accomplishments, challenges, and failures across personal, relationship, and professional domains. Make a 3x3 diagram and have some fun. These grids serve as great visuals in the future when you're wondering "what the hell happened in 2016 again?"

Therapist

What behaviors, habits, people, situations, etc held you back? Where did you feel most alive? Be specific.

Coach

What feedback have you received, and how have you acted upon it? Be specific.

Self

Write yourself an email outlining your goals, thoughts, and intentions for the year and have it auto-sent for exactly one year into the future

And one extra-credit exercise that's really hard, but very effective. The Eulogy. Write your own eulogy! Imagine at least three speakers across family, friends, etc. What do they say about you?
